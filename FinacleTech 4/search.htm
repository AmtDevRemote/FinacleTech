About Finacle tech

Finacle technologies , established in 2001 and certified by U. S. Government's Small Business Administration (SBA) as a small business 8(A) firm on August 17, 2006, provides a full range of professional and business advisory services to commercial and government sectors clients. It was formed to leverage the quality of the experience and innovative thinking of its founders to provide better and more cost effective solutions to its customers. Our professional services provide end-to-end solutions for our clients. The firm offers cost effective quality IT and business consulting solutions that help enterprises lower their cost, increase efficiency and improve its competitive posture in their markets. The focus is on customer satisfaction.
